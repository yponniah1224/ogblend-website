About Ceylon Cinnamon

The True Cinnamon

Ceylon cinnamon, also known as "true cinnamon," is native to Sri Lanka and is considered the finest cinnamon in the world. Unlike common cassia cinnamon, Ceylon cinnamon has a delicate, sweet flavor and contains significantly lower levels of coumarin.
